Don't Let Chuck Norris Kick You In The Face

About the Game

Don't be fooled; Chuck Norris, our country's greatest hero, is nearby. Trust me. Just because you can't see him doesn't mean he isn't there. There's no telling what kind of mood he's in right now, either, so I'd tread lightly if I were you.

It's a well known fact that Chuck is good at kicking stuff, but what you might not know is that Chuck absolutely hates it when he comes up with a random word or phrase about himself and people can't guess all the letters and numbers in the word or phrase. It makes him so mad that when someone guesses a wrong letter enough times, things happen. Painful things.

Chuck has come up with a word/phrase for you that has to do with... well, Chuck. He'll provide the punctuation, but you have to figure out the rest. Use the keyboard to guess the letters or numbers in it, but beware: every time you guess incorrectly, Chuck reveals himself a bit more, and if you guess wrong , he will be upon you... and will most likely kick you in the face.
